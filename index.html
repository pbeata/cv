Introduction

Hi! My name is Paul and I am a research scientist in the field of mechanical and aerospace engineering. I work primarily in R&D in the area of Isogeometric Analysis and Simulation developing toolkits for the simulation of shell-like structures (e.g., aircraft fuselages and rotor blades). I am currently preparing to transition into a full-time role as a data scientist in Miami, Florida by leveraging my previous research experience in academia and industry coupled with a passion for exploring solutions to complex questions relying on big data. I am a motivated problem-solver and a lifelong learner who understands the importance of working in diverse teams to accomplish common goals.
